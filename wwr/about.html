History

Our company started in Northern Canada 25 years ago with one goal on our mind, that goal was to be awesome and offer the best white water rafting exsperience possible. We started a small storefront in Whitecourt alberta with 2 boats and 10 life jackets. Since then we have exspanded to 5 locations all the way down the rockey mountains.
